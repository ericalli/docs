---
title: 'Upload Your Logo'
---

You can upload your own logo to your Siimple site. 

1. Click on your **Site Editor**
2. Click on the ‘**Design**’ button at the top of the page
3. Hover over the field titled **‘Logo’** 
4. Click ‘**Replace**’ - this will give you the option to upload an image from your computer
5. Click ‘**Upload from computer’**
6. Select your logo
7. Hit the **‘Save Changes’** button
