---
title: SEO Settings
---

Your Siimple site comes with built-in SEO that will optimize your site for search engines. The following SEO settings are available:

- Title
- Description
- Image

You can edit and preview these settings by navigating to the `Settings` -> `SEO Settings` tag when ediitng your site. 

<img src="/images/seo-settings.jpg" class="rounded-md border" />

When you deploy your Siimple site, these settings will be used to generate the appropriate meta tags for your site.
